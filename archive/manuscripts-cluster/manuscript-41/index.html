#0041

Name

Colin, Mike, Victor

Narrative:Utopian

Object:Changes Color

Domain:Politics

Description

Cultural Neutralizing Visor. A “utility visor” that will be used by civil servants that are responsible or entrusted with protecting citizens. Notably, this visor will have the effect of negating the predisposition of its wearer. No deference to color or politics of the citizen they protect. This view of all citizens as the same will aid the drive to a utopian society.
