In a not-so-distant future, self-sufficiency is essential to survival & to maintain a sense of identity.  Food, soil & tech are examples of resources controlled by the powers that be! In this world, inner city communities are forced to eat from the leaders of this dystopian until this artifacto.  This device will ensure that everyone has access to fresh food by repurposing their used scrapes to reconstruct the same item in its place.

 

The possibilities are endless with the ability to rebuild organic produce.  This device will inevitably end world hunger & can be used to recover old family pictures or redacted articles from our present.
