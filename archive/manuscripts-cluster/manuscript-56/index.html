Name

Nontsikelelo Mutiti

Narrative:Dystopian

Object:Changes Color

Domain:Environment

Description

White hoodie with camouflage capabilities and moving parts. Sensors that pick up microaggressions. Activated hoodie responds by transforming from a hood to a helmet (with full head coverage) and the intention of the hood has an LCD screen that helps the wearer cope/navigate out of the situation by prompting them a way to respond. the suit develops spikes in this mode to protect the wearer. Interior headphones play soothing sounds. The lining of the suit is silky and soft.
