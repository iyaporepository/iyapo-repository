Narrative:Utopian

Object:Tube

Domain:Health

Thinking about Utopian & health makes me think about the pandemic. To me, love is the strongest medicine. The tube works at two scales, both engaging the body & the planet. The tube collects feelings of love through touch (hugs, holding hands, etc) and this love helps to heal you. You can also share this collective & collected love w/others through touch & give it to others. On a planetary scare, there is a tube that encompasses the world (infrastructure of care), and love can be shared & felt around the world, beyond the bounds of geography.
