#0107

Narrative:Utopian

Object:Transmits Data

Domain:Food

Description

System which connects the hungry and homeless to locally sourced food grown by urban farmers/community gardens. The information is accessed via small, nondescript "nodes" that are placed in usual zones of homelessness. These nodes light up upon pressing a button at its center, then transmitted and informed by geolocation. At each location, self catering tools are supplied.
