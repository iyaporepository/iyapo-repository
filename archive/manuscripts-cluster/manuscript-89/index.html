Description

Space-time varnish. A sealant that protects human bodies during entry and exit to/from the atmosphere. The varnish seals in pockets of oxygen to allow for breathing. The varnish contains nanoparticles of energy that maintain human body temperature in the cold of space or protect from the heat of the sun. It is a painted varnish, therefore inexpensive and accessible to many. Sensors in the varnish collect audio, visual and sensory data for space exploration.
