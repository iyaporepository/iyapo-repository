Name

RaeLeone Allen

Description

In the apocalyptic future, the white patriarchal system has endeavored to ban the people’s production and growth of their own food. Possession of seeds is an arrestable offense. The people cannot be denied and have develop a system of hollow beaded necklaces that hold seeds. The type of seed held in the beads is indicated by the shape and color of the beads. The necklace one wears indicates their status. Flower plant seeds are the most expensive.
