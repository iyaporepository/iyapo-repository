Narrative:Dystopian

This is a pocket-sized drone that helps a plant and dead land come back alive. This contraption can be 3D printed. It came from Dystopian society where lands are dead, and this was made with purpose to revive the dead land. The drone has to be set down on the ground to elevate, and it will use a rhythmic wavelength that the soil reacts to like an enzyme to help it grow faster and gather its nutrients.
