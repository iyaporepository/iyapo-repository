Description

Self defence - our cap allows for people to stove their identity of self and of their communities to draw on during memory lapse - short or long term. People have the capacity to connect their caps to the caps of others to expand their sense of history, of the present, and future. It prevents the loss of self and strengthens their connectedness and reception of self and community.
