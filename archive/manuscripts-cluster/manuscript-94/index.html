Description

Justus seeds. In the future, our food systems fail, due to the impact of Monsanto and agribusiness models. People evolve to survive by growing their own food on their body; the health of the ecosystem depends on the health of the self and the community. Each human garden needs other human garden to thrive. Each individual human's garden takes the form of a hemp-based garment that resembles a tunic. The plants transmit good feelings to each other and what is being.
